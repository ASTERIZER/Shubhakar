Our Mushroom Varieties

Oyster Mushrooms

Delicate, mild flavor with velvety texture. Perfect for stir-fries and soups.

  • Growing Difficulty: Easy
  • Harvest Time: 14-21 days
  • Nutritional Value: High in protein, fiber, and vitamins

Lion's Mane

Known for cognitive benefits and seafood-like flavor. Great for brain health.

  • Growing Difficulty: Moderate
  • Harvest Time: 10-14 days
  • Nutritional Value: Contains compounds that support nerve growth

Shiitake

Rich, earthy flavor and meaty texture for gourmet dishes. A culinary favorite.

  • Growing Difficulty: Moderate
  • Harvest Time: 2-7 months
  • Nutritional Value: Contains lentinan, which boosts immune system
